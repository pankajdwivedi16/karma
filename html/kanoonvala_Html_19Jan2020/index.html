Trademark Registration under Class 11

109 36 56

A person can register a Trademark under the appropriate Trademark Class to protect his/her brand's mark, logo, slogan, image or word. The Trademark Classes in India are divided into 45 classes in accordance with the Nice Classification. The primary importance of these classes is that the Nice Classification is followed worldwide and Trademark registration for a good or service can be done under the same class in different countries.
